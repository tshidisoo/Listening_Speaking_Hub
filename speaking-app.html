🏫 The Classroom!

Grammar: How many…? · There are… · Colours · Possessives

📦 Vocabulary

Say each word OUT LOUD first!

boys girls teachers windows doors
💬 Practise the Dialogues

Read the questions and answers aloud. Try both sides!

❓ How many girls are there?
👦 There are ___ girls.
❓ What colour is his hair?
👦 It's ___.
🎯 Your Speaking Task

Look around your classroom (or imagine one!) and answer these questions in full sentences:

  • How many boys are there in your class?
  • How many girls are there?
  • How many windows are there?
  • How many doors are there?
  • What colour is your teacher's hair?
🗣️ Remember: "There are four boys." — use a full sentence every time!

Day 1 · Unit B 🎤 Speaking ✅ Done!

😊 Feelings!

Grammar: Is he/she…? · No, he/she isn't. · He's/She's…

📦 Vocabulary

Say each feeling OUT LOUD and make the face!

happy 😄 sad 😢 tired 😴 cold 🥶 scared 😨 hungry 🤤
💬 Practise the Dialogues

Read out loud — try to answer with a DIFFERENT feeling each time!

❓ Is he sad?
👦 No, he isn't. He's ___.
❓ Is she cold?
👦 No, she isn't. She's ___.
🎯 Your Speaking Task

Answer these questions about a friend or family member — use your imagination!

  • Is your friend happy? (Say yes or no + the real feeling!)
  • Is your mum tired? (No, she isn't. She's…)
  • Is your teacher scared? (No, he/she isn't. He's/She's…)
  • Are YOU hungry right now?
🗣️ Always give a FULL answer: "No, he isn't. He's happy."

Day 2 · Unit A 🎤 Speaking ✅ Done!

📍 Where Is It?

Grammar: Prepositions of place · Where is…? · It's / They're…

📦 Vocabulary

Say each phrase clearly — these are tricky!

in front of behind next to between
💬 Practise the Dialogues

Read these aloud and really think about where the objects are!

❓ Where's the skateboard?
👦 It's in front of the table.
❓ Where are the books?
👦 They're between the bag and the chair.
🎯 Your Speaking Task

Look at the objects around you RIGHT NOW and describe where they are:

  • Where is your bag? (It's next to / behind / in front of…)
  • Where is your phone or tablet? (It's…)
  • Where is the door? (It's…)
  • Where is the table? (It's between… and …)
🗣️ "The bag is behind the chair." — always start with It's or They're!

Day 2 · Unit B 🎤 Speaking ✅ Done!

🍎 The Fruit Shop!

Grammar: Have you got…? · Yes, we have. / No, we haven't.

📦 Vocabulary

Say each fruit and point to it if you can find one!

🍎 apples 🍌 bananas 🍇 grapes 🍐 pears 🫐 figs 🍊 oranges
💬 Practise the Dialogues

Imagine you're the shopkeeper! Answer the customer's questions.

🛒 Have you got apples?
🧑‍🍳 Yes, we have!
🛒 Have you got figs?
🧑‍🍳 No, we haven't. Sorry!
🎯 Your Speaking Task

You are the shopkeeper! Record yourself answering ALL these customer questions. Decide yourself — YES or NO for each fruit!

  • Have you got bananas?
  • Have you got grapes?
  • Have you got pears?
  • Have you got oranges?
  • Have you got figs?
🗣️ "Yes, we have!" or "No, we haven't." — be a confident shopkeeper!

Day 3 · Unit A 🎤 Speaking ✅ Done!

🏫 School Places!

Grammar: What have we got…? · We've got… · Where are…? · They're…

📦 Vocabulary

Say each word — can you find any of these in YOUR school?

sports field art room computer room football nets whiteboard crayons computers
💬 Practise the Dialogues

Read both sides aloud — be the teacher AND the student!

❓ What have we got in the art room?
👦 We've got crayons and a whiteboard.
❓ Where are the children?
👦 They're in the computer room.
🎯 Your Speaking Task

Think about YOUR school and answer these questions in full sentences:

  • What have you got in your classroom? (We've got…)
  • What have you got in the computer room? (We've got…)
  • Have you got a sports field at your school?
  • Where are the football nets? (They're…)
  • What is your favourite room at school? Why?
🗣️ "We've got computers and a whiteboard in our classroom." — talk about YOUR real school!
